First Steps

Kindergarten is a vital time in a child’s life where they take many important first steps toward independence. These first steps span emotional, social, physical, and cognitive growth. By encouraging children to take on new responsibilities, make choices, and manage their emotions, kindergarten sets the foundation for a lifelong journey of self-reliance, confidence, and resilience. From another point of view kindergarten is typically the first structured institution in a child’s life. It introduces children to an organized environment with rules, routines, and expectations. While family is the first institution in a child’s life, kindergarten is the place where children step out of the home and into a wider social world.
