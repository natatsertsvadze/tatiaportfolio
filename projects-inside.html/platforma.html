Platforma 7

Platforma 7 is a dynamic photography project co-founded by me to foster a thriving community where photographers can connect, grow, and showcase their work. Our mission is to create an inclusive space that supports photographers in their creative journey while addressing the emotional and technical challenges of the profession.
Through a variety of initiatives such as exhibitions, artist talks, workshops, film screenings, and the Photo Fairsinitiative, we aim to cultivate creativity, enhance professional growth, and build a network of supportive peers.
At Platforma 7, we curate diverse photography showcases that offer visibility to artists and spark meaningful conversations. We facilitate intimate discussions where photographers can share their creative processes and the stories behind their images. Additionally, we provide hands-on learning opportunities designed to expand technical skills and encourage exploration of new creative approaches.
Whether you're an established photographer or just starting, Platforma 7 is a place for collaboration, inspiration, and growth.
