About

Visual Artist | Tbilisi, Georgia

I am Tatia Tsertsvadze, a photographer and visual artist based in Tbilisi, Georgia. My work explores the intersection of storytelling and visual expression, blending traditional photography with contemporary mediums such as video, multimedia installations, and darkroom printing.

Over the years, I have showcased my work in numerous exhibitions across Georgia and Europe. My creative practice is deeply rooted in themes of identity, memory, and transformation, inviting viewers to engage with narratives that transcend the visible frame.

As a co-founder of the Union of Photographers "Platforma 7" and a team member at the Tbilisi Photography & Multimedia Museum, I actively contribute to the visual arts community through curatorial projects, artist mentorship, and educational workshops. My commitment to fostering creative exchange has also led me to participate in international art residencies and collaborative projects, expanding my artistic dialogue across borders.

Beyond digital photography, I am deeply engaged in darkroom practices, where I explore the tactile and experimental aspects of analog photography. Whether curating exhibitions, leading workshops, or capturing the essence of everyday life, I remain dedicated to pushing the boundaries of visual storytelling.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me experimenting with new techniques, collaborating with fellow artists, or wandering the streets of Georgia , always seeking the next story to tell.
